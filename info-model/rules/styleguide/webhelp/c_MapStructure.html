Map Structure

Most map structures depend on the specific content in the particular deliverable. You create that structure by combining and nesting map elements as needed. You must begin your map with a title, but all other elements can be interspersed as needed.

  • <map> (mandatory)
    • <title> (mandatory)
    • <topicmeta> (optional)
    • Any number, at any level, and in any order of the following:
      • <topichead>
      • <topicgroup>
      • <mapref>
      • <topicref>
    • <reltable> (optional)
